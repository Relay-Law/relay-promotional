§  The Privacy Doctrine

We never see anything.
By design, not by policy.

I.Inference

Inference is local.

Every AI inference runs on the computer in your office. No prompt, no document, no thought leaves the machine. Verify with your own packet capture.

II.Storage

Storage is local.

Your files stay on your disk in their original location. Relay reads them in place and never copies, syncs, or uploads. Nothing to breach, because nothing is centralized.

III.Training

Training is not on your data.

Your work product is never used to train, fine-tune, or evaluate any model. Telemetry is opt-in, anonymous, and limited to crash reports.

IV.Compliance

Compliance is the default state.

Relay's architecture maps cleanly to ABA Model Rule 1.6 and the analogous state rules. No private-mode toggle to forget.

Enforced in code, not in a contract.
